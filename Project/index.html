Short Bio

My name is Alexandro Garcia, but I go by Alex. I was born June 4th, 1994, in San Diego, California. I've been in retail for the last few years, but have had previously been employed with Vestas Wind Systems A/S and UpWind Solutions, both are servicers of wind turbine. I worked as an IT assistant and as a file clerk in the HR/Training department. I'm currently in the processes of getting my associates in Math at Southwesten College, with the intentions of transferring to San Diego State University to study Electrical Engineering.

Expectation For The Course

I hope this course provides me with the skills and abilities to transition away from retail jobs into jobs in the technology field. I also hope to learn programming to gain a new hobby.
